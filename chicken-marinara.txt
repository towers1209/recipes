Ingredients:
- 3 pieces of perdue chicken breasts
- 16 oz of your favorite italian marinara sauce
- 1/4 cup of grated sharp parmesan cheese

Directions:
1) If the chicken breasts are frozen stiff, soak them in hot water until tender.
2) Once the chicken breasts are tender, dice then into a preferred size.
3) Put the marinara sauce and he chicken breasts in a sauce pan and bring the mix into a boil.
4) Turn down the heat to a simmer.
5) Add tje grated sharp parmesan cheese.
6) Simmer for about 15 minutes.
